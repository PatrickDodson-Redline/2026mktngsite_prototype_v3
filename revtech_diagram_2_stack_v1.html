Where the Agentic OS players sit: Rox, Monaco, 11x, and Artisan vertically integrate across layers 1 through 4, replacing CRM, enrichment, signal detection, and outreach execution with a unified agent-driven platform. The bet is that a single system of AI agents assigned per account can outperform four to six disconnected point solutions.
The Managed Intelligence model: These solutions span layers 2 through 5, building custom signal architecture on top of infrastructure like Clay rather than replacing it. Where Agentic OS players collapse layers into a single product, a Managed Intelligence Partner makes each existing layer more effective by connecting them through a tailored reasoning layer calibrated to the client's specific ICP and competitive landscape.
